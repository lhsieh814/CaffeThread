Slide-out Menu For Quick Access to Workouts


  • Easily show the slide-out menu either from swiping from the left or from the top left side-menu button.
  • High-contrast colors and simple icons make the menu clutter-free. Easy on the eyes and allows for quick navigation.

Create your Personalized Workouts


  • Clear and intuitive user interface.
  • One-click to creating your workout to-do lists.
  • Integrates swipe to edit and delete workouts/exercises.

Customize Your Exercises


  • Add the duration, reps, sets, weight and note to your exercises.
  • Avoid re-entering data by setting the exercise info as default for your selected exericse.

Add Exercises and Start Your Workout!


  • Quick glance to view exercise info.
  • Color-coordinate exercises by type.
  • Click-and-drag to reorder list.
  • Swipe left to edit/delete exercise from workout.

All Your Exercise Info Displayed on One View


  • Nicely formated display of exercise info.
  • Quick glance at the name of previous and next exercise.
  • Simple tap on navigation arrow to rotate between exercises.
  • Activate timer by setting a duration
  • Access notes by tapping the info icon next to the reps/sets/weight.

Integrated Interval Trainer Support


  • Set your duration for warm-up, low interval, high interval, cool-down and repetitions.
  • Save your duration as default for future quick access.
  • Large text and color-coded for each stage.

Integrated Timer and Stopwatch


  • Easily switch between countdown timer and stopwatch functionality.
  • Simple and clutter-free.
